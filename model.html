Introduction

In this assignment we were asked to make a website showing our Web Development CV Site. Using only HTML5 and CSS we were asked to create 6 webpage which are the index page, about page, cv page, social page, model page and contact page. The index page is the first page which is also called a landing page. the about page will give a short description of our biography our professional aspirations. The other requirements were the social page which shows our social sites, the cv page which shows our Curriculum vite, the contact page which includes a form and a map. I my personal choice of website is the minimal kind. I like to keep the content as minimum as possible so that people who are browsing through my website can easily identify what they need to see and need to click. It will also be easy to redirect users to the desired webpage. I researched a lot of website in google, Pinterest and reviews in YouTube and found that the best looking and easy to browse website are those which are minimal in content but very informative.
